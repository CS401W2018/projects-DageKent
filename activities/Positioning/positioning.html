What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal flow means HTML elements follow default approach to list, Block-level element arrange from top to bottom, and Inline element arrange from left to right.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float can let elments get rid of normal flow, and float left or right, usually used for arrange pictures or create layout. Clear the float can prevent prevent floated elements from affect subsequent elements, prevent messive layout.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Absolute means the elements removed from normal flow, and positioned relative to the nearest non-static ancestor. Fixed means relatively positioned browser's window, and not scroll with the page scrolling. Relative means elements still in normal flow, but shift relatie to its original position.
