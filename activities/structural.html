What goes in the header?

The Header contains such as page title, logo, slogan, and sometimes navigation likes.

What goes in the Nav?

The Nav contains navigation links. It will guide users to different section of the same page or different page within the website.

Can the Nav go in the header?

Yes, the Nav can go inside the Header.

Does it have to go in the header?

Not necessary, the Nav can go anywhere on the website page, such as a sidebar or footer.

Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Yes, the Nav can contain links to other websites, but it is primarily used for internal navigation of websites.

What goes in Main?

The Main contains core content of a website page. such as articles, blog posts, images or videos, and text content.

What goes in the Footer?

The Footer contains additional information, such as copyright notice, contact details, social media links, and secondary navigation.
