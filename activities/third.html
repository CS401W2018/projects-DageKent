5.What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL tags are ordered lists. That order can follow all the way to infinite. We can use type="variables" in an OL tag, we can add a letter such as "a" or "A", the number "1", and the Roman number"i" or "I", then the text will show such as upper and lower case a, b, c..., and1, 2, 3..., and i, ii, iii. ol is good for telling steps or lists of rank. UL tags are unordered lists. That has no order. Text in the tag will show a small dot at the beginning of each text. Also, we can change the small dots to different characters. For example. We can use style=" list-style-type: square;" in ul to change the dots to squares. ul is more for shopping lists, and writing down main points.
