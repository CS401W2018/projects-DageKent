Traditional Chinese Festivals

The traditional festivals are essential parts of Chinese culture. In the ancient period, people may worship sky and the land, then people eat dumpling in the new year, and boats raising in the Dragon Boat Festival. Also, there are many festivals that express feeling or wish that people want to reunion. Nowadays,We also helped them apply for World Cultural Heritage. On the other hands, China set up public on the festivals. Even today, these festival are important to Chinese people, because they will remind Chinese people who they are.
