Inroduction of Harbin Ice and Snow World

Harbin International Ice and Snow Sculpture Festival is the biggest winter festival in the northern region of China, the activity attracts thousands of tourists. During the festival, tourists can watch beautiful ice and snow sculptures contain unique designs and skills of artists. Expect ice and snow projects, there are ski and ice entertainments, etc. that variety of projects that bring joy to people to enjoy winter.

At the festival, the night light show adds many dreamy colors for the ice and snow sculptures, it seems like entering a fairy tale world. The festival atmosphere is strong, tourists took many pictures and experienced joyful moments that were brought about by ice and snow. Each year, Harbin is a popular tourist destination because shocking scale and unique artistic charm.
